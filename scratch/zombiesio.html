[==== Zombies.io ====]

The apocalypse has come! Zombies are everywhere! Get loot by breaking crates. Avoid the bloodthirsty zombies. Shoot and kill them! Survive as long as you can!

• Controls

- After the Green Flag is clicked, you have 15 seconds before the zombies start spawning.

- Press WASD or arrow keys to move around

- Press the F key to pickup items.

- Left click or press space to attack.



• Items

- G18C (Glock 18C)

A simple pistol that has a normal fire rate. It can deal 1 damage per shot to zombies. You can also use this to break crates.

- Knife

The starter weapon. It is short and can only deal 1 damage per attack.

- 9mm. Ammo

This is the ammunition that the G18C uses. Make sure to keep track of your ammo.

- Medkit

You can use this to heal yourself by clicking the medkit icon on the bottom right corner.

- Fire Axe

This is my favorite melee weapon. It deals only 1 damage per strike but it has long length. Plus it brings out the apocalypse vibe.

- Mosin Nagant

It has a very slow fire rate but has a high damage of 2 points. It also have a very small ammo capacity of 7. Can also be used to break crates

- 7.62mm Ammo

This is the ammunition that the Mosin Nagant uses.

- Hammer

It gives 1 damage to zombies per attack and can instantly destroy crates in one hit.

- Katana

The strongest weapon in the game. It can deal 2 damage per slash and it is pretty long.
